Mechanical Refrigeration Services (MRS) is a Doral, Florida–based commercial and industrial HVAC contractor specializing in large-scale mechanical systems across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach Counties. From complex duct fabrication and VRF installation to full Design/Build mechanical scopes, we deliver precision engineering for the most demanding commercial projects.

Our clients include luxury retail brands, hospitality groups, government agencies, and department stores — all of whom rely on MRS as their single point of contact for mechanical scope. Based in Doral, FL, we serve general contractors and project owners throughout South Florida.

What We Do

Commercial & Industrial HVAC
Services in South Florida.

MRS handles the full scope of commercial and industrial HVAC work across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach Counties — from engineered duct systems and VRF installation to large-scale Design/Build mechanical projects.

❄️

Commercial HVAC Installation

Complete Design/Build HVAC installation for commercial buildings, retail stores, hotels, and large facilities across South Florida. MRS serves as single point of contact for the GC from submittal through final commissioning.

🔩

Industrial Duct Systems

Custom industrial duct fabrication and installation for high-volume commercial and industrial environments. All ductwork is fabricated in-house to exact specifications, reducing project costs and ensuring on-time delivery.

🏗️

Large-Scale Mechanical Projects

End-to-end mechanical system management for complex multi-phase commercial and industrial construction projects. MRS coordinates directly with general contractors, engineers, and all on-site trades.

🌡️

VRF & Refrigeration Systems

Installation and commissioning of commercial VRF systems and large-scale refrigeration infrastructure. MRS self-performs all VRF work including unit installation, refrigerant piping, duct connections, and controls.

💨

Ventilation & Air Quality

Engineered commercial ventilation solutions for code compliance and optimal indoor air quality. MRS designs and installs ventilation systems for retail stores, office buildings, restaurants, and industrial facilities.

🔧

Sheet Metal Fabrication

In-house commercial sheet metal fabrication for all duct types, fittings, and custom transitions. Send us your drawings and MRS will fabricate to your exact specifications with unparalleled craftsmanship — regardless of size or scope.
